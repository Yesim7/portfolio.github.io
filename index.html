Biography

About Me

PhDCandidate

I completed my BSc in Mathematics (Computer Science emphasis) at Ege University (2020) and my MSc in Computer Science at Ege University (2023). Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Engineering at Izmir Bakırcay University, where I have been working as a Research Assistant since April 2024.

My doctoral studies are centred on distributed algorithm design and its applications in network optimisation. My broader research interests include critical element detection in networks, machine learning for healthcare, and Virtual Reality-based training systems.

Focus

Main Research Areas

01

Critical Element Detection

Designing centralised and distributed algorithms for detecting critical nodes and edges in multi-hop wireless networks to maximise connectivity and resilience.

02

Machine Learning in Healthcare

Prediction of disease severity, survival analysis, and clinical decision support using classical ML and deep learning approaches on real-world medical datasets.

03

Virtual Reality-Based Training

Scenario design and evaluation for occupational safety and campus digitalisation using immersive VR technologies for effective skill acquisition.
